CheckUp

After helping a good friend through the tiresome, complicated process of being diagnosed with Multiple Sclerosis, I decided to design CheckUp. CheckUp is a mobile-first web application designed to ease communication between doctor and patient by allowing users to organize questions pre and post doctor’s appointment, record appointments, track symptoms and store photos and files.
